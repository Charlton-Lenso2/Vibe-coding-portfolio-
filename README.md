## Vibe Coding

This project was **vibe coded** — meaning I collaborated with **Claude AI (by Anthropic)** to design and build the entire portfolio through conversation. I described what I wanted, gave feedback, and directed the creative process while Claude helped generate and refine the code.

Vibe coding is a modern way of building projects where you focus on the **vision, design, and content** while using AI as your coding partner. It's fast, creative, and a great way to bring ideas to life — especially for students and entrepreneurs who are still learning.

> *"I directed it. AI assisted it. The ideas, the content, and the vision are entirely mine."*
